### The thesis

A number is what's left once you forget the relation that produced it. Most of what we call worth тАФ money, attention, meaning тАФ is a scalar standing in for a structure it can no longer remember. My work is the slow project of putting the structure back: currencies that are vectors rather than scalars, proofs gated on *coherence* rather than count, and AI treated not as a tool in the corner of the workshop but as the other half of the conversation the workshop exists to have.

I ship in the open. Some of it holds; all of it is honest.
