### the thesis

I build things that test a single suspicion: **value is not a quantity you measure but an agreement that holds**.

A number is what's left over once you forget the relation that produced it. Most of what we call worth — money, attention, meaning — is a scalar standing in for a structure it can no longer remember. My work is the slow project of putting the structure back.

So the currencies here are vectors, not scalars. The proofs are gated on *coherence* rather than count. The games have economies and the economies have cosmologies. And the AI isn't a tool in the corner of the workshop — it's the other half of the conversation that the workshop exists to have.
